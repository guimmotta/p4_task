All about Split

Bizzare

With some of the the most uncomfortable scenes you can find out there, this will make you wish no one walks in while you are watching.

Ominous

Probably with the best fighting back is useless scene that I can think of. The kind of horror that will make you shiver.

Action

Yes, you could try to fight back, But it wouldn't take you anywhere. But yet, no horror can leave action out.

Overall, Perfect

The re-re-watchable kind of movie. It really honors the Horror Genre. No cheap jumpscare bullshit, this the real stuff.

About the Plot

Kevin, who is suffering from dissociative identity disorder and has 23 alter egos, kidnaps three teenagers. They must figure out his friendly personas before he unleashes his 24th personality.
